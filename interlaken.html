High lights

Tandem Paraglide

Tandem paragliding in Interlaken is an unforgettable way to soar above the majestic Swiss Alps and the twin lakes, Thun and Brienz. Most flights depart from Beatenberg (around 1,350m above sea level) and glide 5 to 20 minutes down to Höhematte Park in central Interlaken, offering stunning views of the Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau mountains

Grindelwald

Grindelwald is a picturesque mountain village nestled in the Bernese Oberland region of Switzerland, surrounded by the stunning peaks of the Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau mountains. Known as the "Glacier Village," it offers breathtaking alpine scenery, charming Swiss chalets, and a wide range of outdoor activities throughout the year. In summer, visitors enjoy hiking, biking, and scenic gondola rides, while winter transforms Grindelwald into a ski and snowboarding paradise. The village also serves as a gateway to the famous Jungfraujoch - the "Top of Europe." With its blend of adventure, natural beauty, and traditional Swiss charm, Grindelwald is a dream destination for nature lovers and explorers alike

River rafting

River rafting on the Lütschine River in the Bernese Oberland is an exhilarating adventure that combines the thrill of white-water rapids with breathtaking Alpine scenery. Flowing from the base of the Eiger's north face, the river offers exciting Class III-IV rapids, making it one of Switzerland's top rafting destinations. Guided by professional instructors, participants navigate through fast currents and swirling waters while enjoying views of lush forests and snow-capped peaks. The experience typically starts near Grindelwald and ends near Lake Brienz, with all gear and safety equipment provided. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast or a first-time rafter, Lütschine rafting offers a fun, safe, and unforgettable way to explore the natural beauty of the Swiss Alps.
